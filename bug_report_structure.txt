BUG REPORT STRUCTURE

1. identifier	unique
	
2. summary 	brief description of the problem in one sentence; 
		at least, it should explain what happened, where and under what conditions. 

3. description	detailed description of the defect with info about expected and actual results,
		link to requirements.

4. STR		steps to reproduce 

5. reproducibility	always/sometimes

6. severity 	critical/major/minor/trivial. 
		Degree of damage to product perfomance (operability)

7. priority 	ASAP/high/normal/low. How quickly the deffect should be fixed. 
		Degree of influence on business processes and usability. 

8. workaround 	bypass the defect
 
9. comments	additional info

10. attachments	

11. Environment
